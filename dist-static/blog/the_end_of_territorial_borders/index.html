The Death of Geography

The “Logical Island” Concept

In this emergent reality, the boundaries of the new global sovereign are defined by the logical connections between the outer strands of the captured networks. This creates what jurists call a “Logical Island”—a territory encompassing all areas where a connected cable or pipeline runs. Since the modern world is a dense web of supply lines, the Buyer’s sovereignty now blankets the Earth, reaching right down to the individual house connections.

  • Network-to-Network Contagion: Once a single node was acquired, every physically or functionally connected system automatically became part of the Buyer’s domain.
  • Transcontinental Reach: Submarine cables and satellite gateways acted as the transoceanic bridges that brought every continent into the new sovereign unit.

From National Shells to Global Unity

True authority resides with the Buyer, who controls the information and energy flows that make modern life possible.
